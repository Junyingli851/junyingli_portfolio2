Pet Medical

[ Background ]

As the number of users who keep pets increases, the market for pets also becomes larger, and there may be 2-3 pet hospitals in the same block. Therefore, how to choose the store that best meets the needs of users from many stores is a problem.

[ Current Status]

Users do not have fifixed pet hospital-related software, and they are used to searching for pet hospitals with Google Maps, Yelp, and other third companies., then researching and comparing other user reviews and published photos in multiple software, which takes users a lot of time.

[ Initial Problem Discovery ]

Users need to spend a lot of time choosing a suitable pet hospital from multiple apps.
